Who Can Play

  • Open division, male and female players, ages 16 and older.
  • Teams of five: three on the court, two substitutes.
  • Two-day commitment — Saturday pool play and Sunday brackets (Aug 15–16).
Players aged 16–17

Minors may play, with two requirements: (1) a parent or guardian must sign the liability waiver, and (2) the player must receive Commissioner / committee approval before the event. Bring the signed waiver to check-in.

How to Register

  1. Submit your team through the registration form — team name, captain contact, five players, and shirt sizes.
  2. Pay the $250 entry (payment method confirmed at registration).
  3. Every player signs the Liability Waiver and Code of Conduct. Players 16–17 need a parent/guardian signature on the waiver.
  4. Bring signed forms and your roster to check-in the morning of Day 1.

What to Bring

  • Signed liability waiver for every player (parent/guardian signature for anyone 16–17).
  • Signed Code of Conduct.
  • Your five-player roster and captain contact.

Where It Goes

$1,000 each to Blair High School, Pasadena Poly, and John Muir High School — with John Muir's gift seeding a scholarship fund in Pervis Polk's name. Family. Community. Competition.

Rules of play (including the "Cal Tech 17" format) are on the Rules & Conduct page. Everything is compiled in the Event Bible.
