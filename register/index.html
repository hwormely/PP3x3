Eligibility & Rules

  • Open division — 18+ only
  • 3–5 players per roster (3 on court, 1–2 subs)
  • FIBA 3×3 rules apply (21-point cap, 10-min game clock, 12-sec shot clock)
  • Both days are mandatory — pool play Saturday, bracket Sunday
  • Day 1: 10 pools of 5, full round-robin, 4 guaranteed games
  • Day 2: Championship (seeds 1–16), Consolation (17–32), Community (33–50)
  • $250 entry fee due at registration to secure your spot
  • Teams must check in by 8:00 AM on Saturday
  • Rosters are locked at check-in — no additions after first game
